Howard University · Chirality, Topological Materials, Nonlinear Transport & Optical Responses

Hello! I am Ashutosh. I work on chirality-driven quantum phenomena and topological materials, focusing on symmetry-governed nonlinear transport and optical responses using first-principles electronic-structure methods.

My research connects symmetry, Berry curvature physics, and band topology to measurable response functions in quantum materials.
