Howard University · Chirality, Topological Materials, Nonlinear Transport & Optical Responses

Hello! I’m Ashutosh. Welcome to my homepage. I am interested in chirality-driven quantum phenomena and topological materials, with an emphasis on symmetry-governed nonlinear transport and optical responses.

I work primarily with first-principles electronic-structure methods (DFT and related tools), and connect symmetry, Berry-phase physics, and electronic structure to measurable response functions.

Research

My research focuses on (i) chiral quantum materials, (ii) topological band structures and Berry curvature physics, (iii) nonlinear transport (e.g., nonlinear Hall/rectification), and (iv) optical responses (linear and nonlinear).
